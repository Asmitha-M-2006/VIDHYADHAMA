Eligibility Criteria

Completion of 10th Grade

Successful completion of SSLC/CBSE/ICSE or equivalent from a recognized board is mandatory.

Academic Performance

Applicants must achieve the minimum academic performance requirement as specified by the college board during counseling.

Available Streams

Admissions are open for Science (PCMB, PCMC, PCME) and Commerce (CEBA, SEBA, HEBA) streams.

Admission Process

Step 1

Submit Enquiry / Application

Fill out our online enquiry form or submit a physical application at our campus admissions office.

Step 2

Counselling / Stream Selection

Attend a personalized counselling session to determine the best stream based on your academic goals.

Step 3

Document Verification

Submit all necessary original documents for verification by the academic committee.

Step 4

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Finalize your admission by completing the fee payment and receiving your official enrollment letter.
