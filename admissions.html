Eligibility Criteria

Completion of 10th Grade

Successful completion of SSLC/CBSE/ICSE or equivalent from a recognized board is mandatory.

Academic Performance

Applicants must meet the academic requirements specified by the college during counselling.

Available Streams

Admissions are open for Science (PCMC, PCMB) and Commerce (SEBA, CEBA) streams.

Admission Process

Step 1

Submit Enquiry / Application

Fill out our online enquiry form or submit a physical application at our campus admissions office.

Step 2

Counselling / Stream Selection

Attend a personalised counselling session to select the stream best aligned with your academic goals.

Step 3

Document Verification

Submit all necessary original documents for verification by the academic committee.

Step 4

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Confirm your admission by completing the fee payment and receiving the official enrolment confirmation.
