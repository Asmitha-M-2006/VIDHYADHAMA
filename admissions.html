Eligibility Criteria

Completed 10th Grade

Successful completion of SSLC/CBSE/ICSE or equivalent from a recognized board is mandatory.

Minimum Percentage

Applicants must achieve the required aggregate percentage in their 10th grade (details provided during counseling).

Science & Commerce Streams

Admissions open for students aspiring for Science (PCMB, PCMC, PCME) and Commerce (CEBA, SEBA, HEBA) streams.

Commitment to Discipline

We welcome students who are committed to academic discipline and holistic personal growth.

Our Simple Admission Process

Step 1

Submit Enquiry

Fill out our online enquiry form or visit our campus for initial information and guidance.

Step 2

Campus Visit & Counseling

Explore our state-of-the-art facilities and receive personalized academic counseling.

Step 3

Document Verification

Submit all required documents for thorough verification and complete the official application form.

Step 4

Admission Confirmation

Secure your seat upon successful verification of documents and completion of fee payment.

Documents Required for Admission

Original SSLC/10th Marks Card (with photocopies)
Original Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous institution
Two recent passport-size photographs
Copy of Aadhaar Card or other valid ID proof
Migration Certificate (for non-Karnataka students)
Caste/Income Certificate (if applicable)
